Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

This strawberry-banana protein smoothie with almonds and protein powder is great for a meal replacement or after a workout.

This recipe have a prep time of 10 minutes and Serving size for 1 person.

Nutrition Facts(per Serving):

  • Calories: 349
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 53g
  • Protein: 21g

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups sliced fresh Strawberries
  • 1 Banana
  • 10 whole almonds
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 tbsp chocolate flavored protein powder

Steps

  1. Begin by assembling strawberries, banana, almonds, and water within the confines of your blender.
  2. Execute the blending process until all ingredients amalgamate seamlessly. Introduce ice cubes into the blend and continue the blending process until a velvety smooth texture is achieved.
  3. Integrate the protein powder into the concoction and continue blending for an additional 30 seconds, ensuring uniform distribution throughout the mixture. This step will enhance the nutritional value of your beverage while contributing to its overall creaminess and taste.
